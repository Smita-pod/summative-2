Installation

About Installation and Exhibition in your City

Once you make your art piece, take a pic and post on our website. Write a brief of your process of creating, inspiration behind and the experience of. And then keep your masterpiece safe for display later.

Once we get through this Pandemic. Each city will have a massive exhibition display where all the participants can exhibit their art piece and will be displayed there permanently forever.

This way we will create a lill memorial of this Global Pandemic. A form of creating a “Good Memory for Bad times.”
