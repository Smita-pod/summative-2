About Us

About Nature's Atelier

Nature’s Atelier is New-zealand based Art school. It provides Nature based Arts and craft learning experiences to kids and Adults.

Nature’s Atelier defines arts-based environmental education as a form of learning that aims to develop environmental understanding and responsibility in kids right from the young age.

About Rubbish Art Festival

Nature’s Atelier Organising a family Art event to help people deal with Isolation and have some fun. As we can’t go outside because of the lockdown, it is an online Art event where you can participate through the Website staying safe at home and make something with the Rubbish and share your creativity with the world.

It can be a small but united gesture to show our fortitude against this global enemy named “Coronavirus”.

The idea is to make families celebrate this quarantine time by spending some creative time with their kids and partners laughing, brainstorming, innovating and creating your materpiece, with nothing but the Trash at home.

At this event website, Participants can register and upload a pic of their artwork and share with the world.
