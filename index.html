What’s Your

Creation In Isolation

Rubbish Art Festival is a family Art event organised by Nature’s Atelier Art Studio to help people deal with Isolation and have some fun.

As we can’t go outside because of the lockdown, it is an online Art event where you can participate through the Website staying safe at home and make something with the Rubbish and share your creativity with the world. It can be a small but united gesture to show our fortitude against this global enemy named “Coronavirus”.

The idea is to make families celebrate this quarantine time by spending some creative time with their kids and partners laughing, brainstorming, innovating and creating your materpiece, with nothing but the Trash at home. At this event website, Participants can register and upload a pic of their artwork and share with the world.

What You Can Use?

Participants can use anything useless available at their homes.Have a walk in your garden, and pick up any loose materials from the Nature.Clutter in all the useless recyclable resources from your store area or garage and you are all set to go.

About Installation

Once we get through this Pandemic. Each city will have a massive exhibition display where all the participants can exhibit their art piece and will be displayed there permanently forever. This way we will create a lill memorial of this Global Pandemic. A form of creating a “Good Memory for Bad times.”
